About Sportz

Living a healthy life in harmony with nature and our own inner needs is the biggest challenge facing every individual in this world.

This is where we come in, with the Best quality Turfs. We, at Sportz, are striving to bring all the aspect of healthy living to you through our active sports engagement, healthy dietary habits and social gatherings. This is what we are, a Melting Pot of Everything Healthy.

A few rules you must follow while entering the turf.

  • ONLY Sportswear should be worn while playing on the turf.
  • ONLY Football or Sports shoes should be worn while playing on the turf.
  • NO FOOD & DRINKS are allowed inside the turf.
  • Do not damage Sportz property
  • SPORTSMAN SPIRIT to be maintained during games.
  • NO FIGHTS will be allowed within the Sportz premises.
  • NO SMOKING inside the turf.
  • DO NOT LITTER anywhere inside the Sportz premises including the turf before or after the your game.
